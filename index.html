Moin.

I am a postdoctoral researcher and deputy head of the Arbeitsgruppe Numerik partieller Differentialgleichungen, Technische Universität Chemnitz, lead by Roland Herzog, which I joined in April 2018. Previously I worked within the Image Processing Group, Technische Universität Kaiseslautern, led by Gabriele Steidl, where I finished my habilitation beginning of 2018. Prior to that I was a doctoral researcher and teaching assistant at the Institut für Mathematik, Universität zu Lübeck, working together with my PhD supervisor Jürgen Prestin.

Currently I am working on

  • nonsmooth optimization on manifolds, especially proximal methods and their application in manifold-valued image processing

  • optimization methods on manifolds and high-dimensional power manifolds

  • graph based methods for manifold-valued image and data processing combining both local and nonlocal methods.

  • anisotropic periodic translation invariant spaces and Fourier based algorithms
